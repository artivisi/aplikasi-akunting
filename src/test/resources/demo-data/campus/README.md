# Demo Data: STMIK Merdeka Digital

Private college (Sekolah Tinggi Manajemen Informatika dan Komputer) in Yogyakarta.
Two study programs: S1 Informatika and S1 Sistem Informasi.
Established 2005, non-PKP educational institution under Yayasan Merdeka Digital.

8 employees, ~100 transactions in 2025 + 2026 Q1.
Student cohorts: Angkatan 2023 (120 students), 2024 (120), 2025 (120).
Revenue from tuition (SPP), registration fees (uang pangkal), lab fees (praktikum), grants (hibah), and foundation funding.
